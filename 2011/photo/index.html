from Brad Cerasani

Lead Photographer

What began as a supplement to our design services quickly became a cornerstone of our business. I approach photography with a graphic designer’s eye for composition and the processing proficiency of someone who worked professionally in Photoshop before ever owning a camera. Having logged over 20 000 miles in 2011 alone, my work has appeared in everything from international trade show displays to industry leading magazines.

Promotional/Advertising

To create photos that sell we take care to review goals, message, and intent before ever lifting a camera. Our collaborative approach ensures your message is instilled in our images from brainstorming and conception to editing and delivery.

Architectural

In architectural photography, balancing a myriad of light sources requires great expertise to be executed successfully. Our experience has brought us everywhere from modern open-concept bungalows to sprawling 10 000 sq ft luxury estates. With over 94% of home buyers beginning their real-estate search online, commissioning professional photographs of your property is one of the most important investments you’ll make.

Event

Event photography is all about telling a story, and in this genre we adopt a photojournalist’s approach. We employ wide angle prime (fixed focal length) lenses to put the viewer in the emotion and atmosphere, as opposed to using longer telephoto lenses to “snipe” only fragments of the experience. Professional, personable photographers and some of the finest low-light optics commercially available ensure memories from your event are preserved in their best light.
